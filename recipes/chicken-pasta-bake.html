Chicken Pasta Bake

Description

Enjoy this gooey cheese and chicken pasta bake for the ultimate weekend family dinner. Serve Straight from the dish with a dressed green salad.

Ingredients
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1/4 tsp chilli flakes
  • 2 x 400g cans chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tsp caster sugar
  • 6 tbsp marscapone
  • 4 skinless chicken breasts, sliced into strips
  • 300g penne
  • 70g mature cheddar
  • 50g grated mozarella
  • small bunch of parsley, finely chopped
